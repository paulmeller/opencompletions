# Visa Category Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure completeness. For each category, search for whether the target country offers one or more visa types. Many countries have multiple visas per category.

## Short-Stay / Visitor
- [ ] Tourist visa
- [ ] Business visitor visa
- [ ] Transit visa
- [ ] Medical treatment visa
- [ ] Conference / event visa

## Work
- [ ] Standard employment visa (employer-sponsored)
- [ ] Highly qualified / skilled worker visa
- [ ] Seasonal worker visa
- [ ] Au pair visa
- [ ] Intra-company transfer (ICT)
- [ ] EU Blue Card (if EU country)
- [ ] Tech / innovation sector visa

## Self-Employment & Business
- [ ] Freelancer / independent professional visa
- [ ] Entrepreneur visa
- [ ] Startup visa
- [ ] Investor / business establishment visa

## Investment & Wealth
- [ ] Golden visa / residence by investment
- [ ] Retirement / passive income / financially independent visa

## Digital & Remote
- [ ] Digital nomad visa
- [ ] Remote worker visa

## Education & Research
- [ ] Student visa (degree programs)
- [ ] Student exchange / short course visa
- [ ] Research / scientific visa
- [ ] Academic teaching visa
- [ ] Professional training / internship visa (paid and unpaid)

## Family
- [ ] Family reunification visa
- [ ] Spouse / partner visa
- [ ] Dependent child visa
- [ ] Dependent parent visa

## Humanitarian & Special
- [ ] Humanitarian visa
- [ ] Asylum / refugee status
- [ ] Victims of trafficking visa
- [ ] Religious purposes visa

## Youth & Cultural
- [ ] Youth mobility / working holiday visa
- [ ] Voluntary work visa
- [ ] Cultural exchange visa

## Other
- [ ] Job seeker visa
- [ ] Retiree visa (if separate from passive income)
- [ ] Self-support / independent means visa
- [ ] Country-specific programs (e.g., ancestry visa, DAFT, treaty investor)

## Verification

After completing your research, search for:
- "[country] new visa types 2025 2026"
- "[country] complete list of visa categories"
- "[country] immigration authority visa types"

Confirm you haven't missed any recently introduced or niche visa programs.
